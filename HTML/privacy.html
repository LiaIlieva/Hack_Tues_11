Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 20.03.2025

1. Introduction

NutriScan ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our services, including our website and mobile application.

2. Data We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account details: Name, email, age, weight, height, dietary preferences
  • Health metrics: Fitness goals, allergies, medical conditions (optional)
  • Barcode scan history: Products scanned and dietary insights

Automated Data

  • Device information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, session duration
  • Cookies: For functionality and analytics (see Cookie Policy)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide personalized nutrition recommendations
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Communicate service updates or security alerts
  • Aggregate anonymized data for research

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share it with:

  • Trusted third-party service providers (e.g., cloud storage, analytics)
  • Legal authorities if required by law
  • New owners in case of business transfer (e.g., merger)

5. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and data
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@nutriscan.com.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Remember login sessions
  • Analyze traffic patterns
  • Personalize content

Manage preferences via your browser settings.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard measures including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited employee access to sensitive data

8. Children's Privacy

NutriScan is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
